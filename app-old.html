A while back, I was watching my son play 'War' with my wife, and it got me thinking about how it might be fun to integrate this game with something else he loves: Pokemon. However, what I thought would be a simple project ended up being one of the more challenging AND rewarding tasks I've taken upon myself.

One of the stipulations I put on myself is I didn't want to rely on any frameworks or libraries like React, Vue, Bootstrap, etc. I have no qualms with any of them; in fact, I work with Vue on a daily basis for work. However, I know it's easy to rely on their conveniences, and I wanted to see what I could do with Vanilla JavaScript, along with jQuery.

In the end, I'm happy with this project, but more importantly, so is my son, and that's really what matters.

Hope you enjoy the game!

-David
